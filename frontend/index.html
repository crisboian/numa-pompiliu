02 · The Method

Five protocols, one continuous cycle.

NUMA structures expert knowledge transfer as a reproducible pipeline — from initial elicitation through long-term maintenance, every step is specified and measurable.

I

Capture

A structured 3.5-hour LLM-guided interview elicits tacit knowledge through adaptive questioning.

3.5h interview
II

Structure

Knowledge is decomposed into a 3-tier ontology: Facts (objective), Judgments (reasoned), Intuitions (heuristic).

3-tier ontology
III

Validation

A canonical 20-question battery probes coverage, contradictions, and confidence calibration.

20 questions
IV

Access

Graph-grounded retrieval with Reciprocal Rank Fusion (k = 60) unifies dense and sparse signals.

RRF · k=60
V

Maintenance

A 6-month refresh cycle keeps the knowledge graph aligned with evolving practice and feedback.

6-month cycle
VI

Negative Knowledge

A dedicated 30-minute phase for costly mistakes, anti-patterns, and safety-critical warnings that no manual records.

Safety-critical
