Security & Privacy

How NUMA handles your data — transparently, openly, and by design.

Our Philosophy

Your data stays yours. NUMA is designed so that captured knowledge lives in your Gmail account, not on our servers. We don't warehouse, mine, or monetize your expertise. The code is open source — anyone can verify exactly what the system does.

What We Collect & Why

Google Account (OAuth)

When you sign in with Google, we receive your email address and name. This is used exclusively to:

  • Create and maintain your session
  • Store captured knowledge as Gmail drafts in your own account
  • Identify ownership of purchases (for report access)

We request the gmail.compose and gmail.labels scopes to save knowledge items as drafts and organize them under a NUMA label in your inbox. We cannot read your other emails.

Payment Information (Stripe)

All payments are handled entirely by Stripe. We never see, store, or process your credit card details. Stripe's security and compliance (PCI DSS Level 1) apply directly.

Captured Knowledge

Interview transcripts, entities, relationships, and knowledge items are stored as Gmail drafts in your account. NUMA's servers never hold a persistent copy — we only cache data temporarily during the active session for performance.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • ❌ IP addresses (no logging of request origins)
  • ❌ Browser fingerprints or cookies beyond the essential session cookie
  • ❌ Analytics, tracking pixels, or telemetry
  • ❌ Third-party data sharing of any kind

GDPR Compliance

NUMA is committed to the General Data Protection Regulation (EU 2016/679). As an open-source project operated by an independent researcher, we follow the regulation's principles in good faith:

  • Right to Access — Your knowledge is stored in your own Gmail drafts. You can read every item at any time.
  • Right to Erasure — Delete your Gmail drafts and your NUMA label. Contact us to remove your account reference from our purchase records.
  • Data Portability — Export your knowledge as Markdown, JSON, or GraphML from the capture interface.
  • Data Minimisation — We collect only what is strictly necessary: your email for authentication and the knowledge you choose to share.
  • Purpose Limitation — Data is used only for the knowledge capture and retrieval you explicitly request.

For any GDPR request, contact: cristianboian@gmail.com

Security Measures

🔒 HTTPS / TLS 1.3 🔑 OAuth 2.0 / OpenID 📜 CSP Headers 🛡️ HSTS Preload 📦 Open Source
  • Transport security: All traffic is served over HTTPS with TLS 1.3. Strict Transport Security (HSTS) is enabled with preload.
  • Authentication: Google OAuth 2.0 with OpenID Connect. Sessions are managed via signed, HTTP-only cookies.
  • Content Security Policy: Strict CSP headers limit script and resource origins, mitigating XSS attacks.
  • No data persistence on server: Knowledge items are stored in your Gmail drafts. Server-side storage is limited to temporary session state and purchase records (anonymised).
  • Open source transparency: The entire NUMA codebase is available under the MIT license. Anyone can audit, fork, or self-host the system.
